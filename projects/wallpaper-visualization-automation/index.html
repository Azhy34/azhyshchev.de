Virtual Wallpaper Try-On

A wallpaper manufacturer's sales team was spending 4-6 hours daily creating client visualizations in Photoshop — manual perspective correction, lighting adjustments, pattern alignment for every single order. Built a production web app that replaces this with a 2-minute AI-generation workflow. 75% manual work reduction from day one.

Engineering decisions

Why AI generation won

  • Prototyped two approaches: geometric transformation (SAM2 segmentation) vs. AI generation. AI won — not because it's more sophisticated, but because non-technical sales staff actually use it.
  • Gemini multimodal handles perspective, lighting, and pattern placement automatically — no masks or corrections needed.
  • Mobile-first design: sales team uses phones on client visits, not laptops.
  • Multi-office billing: client-side API key injection for separate office quotas (Poland / Ukraine).
Business impact

What changed

  • Unlimited parallel mockup processing — no longer limited by one designer's availability.
  • Same-day client delivery replaced the 2-3 day turnaround.
  • 85-90% daily adoption from day one — adoption over elegance.
  • Sales team can now show clients visualizations during the first call, not days later.
